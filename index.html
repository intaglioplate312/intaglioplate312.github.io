About Me

An artist to my core, I’m thrilled to deliver client collateral that converges on the fine- and graphic arts.

Commercially a print production technician and graphic artist, my open and fluid artistic applications take in printmaking and photography. Recently, I have expanded my practice to align with the maker movement, applying lasers, CNC routers and 3D printers to achieve high-level effects to develop engraving, one-of-kind objects and wearables.

My professional development mirrors my art practice, as both are in a continual state of creative flux. Clients are well-served by my varied background in visual communications, from book design to training development.

One of my professional streams is creating web sites for a diverse array of clients. To prepare for this work, I successfully completed a web design certificate at the School of the Art Institute of Chicago in 2011. Currently, I’m augmenting my web developer skills in a master class, Northwestern Coding Boot Camp.
